Our Heritage


A pip Told in Dough
& Flame

Born in the cobblestoned streets of Milan, La Strada carries a singular obsession: that great pizza is not fast food — it is slow art. Each base is fermented for 72 hours. Each topping is sourced from the same Italian producers our founder Enzo Caruso shook hands with in 1987.

We brought that tradition to the heart of the city, and we've never compromised a single ingredient since.

The Philosophy

Only the
Finest Shall
Touch Our Dough

We believe great pizza begins not at the oven, but at the source. Every ingredient is selected by hand, imported weekly, and stored with obsessive care.

  • Caputo 00 Flour — Milled in Naples since 1924
  • San Marzano Tomatoes — DOP certified, Campania
  • Fior di Latte — Whole-milk mozzarella, Agerola
  • Olive Oil — Cold-pressed, Nocellara del Belice
  • Black Truffle — Seasonal, Norcia, Umbria
  • Prosciutto di Parma — 24-month aged, PDO
